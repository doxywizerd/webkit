35 // The JSC concurrent GC sometimes stops the world in order to stay ahead of it. These deliberate,
36 // synthetic pauses ensure that the GC won't have to do one huge pause in order to catch up to the
37 // retreating wavefront. The scheduler is called "space-time" because it links the amount of time
38 // that the world is paused for to the amount of space that the world allocated since the GC cycle
39 // began.
